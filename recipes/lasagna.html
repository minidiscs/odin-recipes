Lasagna



Description

A mouthwatering bake comprised of alternating layers of silky bechamel, tangy marinara, and toothsome flat noodles. It can be made with a red-meat bolognase for a hearty meal, or layered with copious amounts of sauteed spinich for guiltless pleasure.

Ingrediants

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Spinach
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Oregeno
  • Bay Leaf
  • Crushed Red Pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Soak noodles in cool water for 30 minutes, drain and set aside
  2. Meanwhile, add diced onion to two tablespoons of hot oil in medium saucepan over medium heat
  3. Once onion is wilted (about 15 minutes), add garlic and fry 2 minutes
  4. Add red pepper, bay leaf, and oregeno to saucepan. Fry for 1 additional minute
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, stir and bring to simmer. Simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Fry flour with butter in small saucepan until fragrant, but before it takes color. Add milk and nutmeg. Salt to taste. Wisk until smooth while you bring to a simmer and thickens. Set aside.
  7. Sautee spinach with olive oil, salt and pepper
  8. Fill cassarole dish with alternating layers of red sauce, noodles, bechamel and spinach.
  9. Bake at 375 for 60 minutes. Allow to cool for 20 minutes and then serve.
