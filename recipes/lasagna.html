Lasagna

Serves 4 - 6


Description

A mouthwatering bake comprised of alternating layers of silky bechamel, tangy marinara, and toothsome flat noodles. It can be made with a red-meat bolognase for a hearty meal, or layered with copious amounts of sauteed spinich for guiltless pleasure. Many purists believe that a lasagna is not a lasagna without nutmeg laced bechamel. After a pan of this recipe, we think you'll agree!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Lasagna Noodles
  • 1/2 cup Flour
  • 1 & 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1 cup Sauteed Spinach
  • 2 large cans Crushed Tomatoes
  • 3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 3 cloves Garlic
  • 1 large Onion
  • 1 teaspoon Oregeno
  • 1 tablespoon Parm
  • 3 Bay Leaf
  • 1 teaspoon Crushed Red Pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Soak noodles in cool water for 30 minutes, drain and set aside
  2. Meanwhile, add diced onion to two tablespoons of hot oil in medium saucepan over medium heat
  3. Once onion is wilted (about 15 minutes), add garlic and fry 2 minutes
  4. Add red pepper, bay leaf, and oregeno to saucepan. Fry for 1 additional minute
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, stir and bring to simmer. Simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Fry flour with butter in small saucepan until fragrant, but before it takes color. Add milk and nutmeg. Salt to taste. Wisk until smooth while you bring to a simmer and thickens. Set aside.
  7. Sautee spinach with olive oil, salt and pepper
  8. Fill cassarole dish with alternating layers of red sauce, noodles, bechamel and spinach.
  9. Bake at 375 for 60 minutes. Allow to cool for 20 minutes, top with a lil parm and then serve.
