Black Bean Soup

Serves 4 - 6


Description

Black bean soup is low effort, but big on flavor and satisfaction. It takes a while to simmer, so plan ahead. The chipotle peppers gives us a rich spicy smokey flavor without the use of smoked meat. Be sure to cook the beans straight from their dry state to create a rich bean broth. When serving you can add all manner of toppings. Serve with a sandwich for a classic lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb dry black beans
  • 1 large spanish onion
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp refined oil
  • 3 cloves chopped garlic
  • 1/2 can chipotle peppers in adobo
  • salt to taste
  • vinegar to taste

Steps

  1. Saut√© chopped onion in oil over medium head for 10 minutes stiring occasionally
  2. When translucent and slightly browed, add garlic and fry for 1 minute. Add chipotle peppers and fry for 2 minutes
  3. Add 8 cups of water, bay leaves and black beans. Bring to boil and salt to taste.
  4. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 2 hours or until beans are tender
  5. Add a splash of vinegar to taste and serve with sour cream, tortilla, diced onion, radish and cabbage.
