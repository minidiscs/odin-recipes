Cauliflower Soup

Serves 4 - 6


Description

You'll know there's only 2 primary ingredients to this soup, but you'll be forgetting that as soon as you sit down at the table for a bowl. You'll have guests think you're lying about it and vegans convinced you're poisoning them with a rich meat based stock, but yes, there's only two ingredients. There's much more flavor going on that you'll be pinching yourself in disbelief. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, roughly chopped
  • 1 large spanish onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • freshly cracked pepper
  • extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Saut√© chopped onion in oil over medium head for 10 minutes stirring occasionally
  2. When onion is translucent, add cauliflower, 1 cup of water.
  3. Bring to boil, cover and steam cauliflower until tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add 6 cups of water, and use immersion blender to smooth soup
  5. Bring back to a boil, remove from the heat, salt to taste and serve with plenty of evoo and cracked pepper
