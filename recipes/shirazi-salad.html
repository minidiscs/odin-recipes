Shirazi Salad

Serves 14 - 18


Description

Bright, vibrant and refreshing. This salad is known as Salad-e Shirazi in Iran and is a ubiquitous pairing with an Iranian meal. It's made with simple and fresh ingredients and requires little prep. It's very important to get good quality English or Persian cucumbers, fresh crisp tomatoes, and onion. Be sure to chop all the vegetables into cubes roughly the same size.

Ingredients

  • 3 large English cucumbers or 12 smaller Persian cucumbers
  • 8 roma tomatoes or 5 large tomatoes
  • 2 fresh red onion
  • 3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • One handful of chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • Juice of 4 lemons
  • Salt
  • Freshly cracked black pepper

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables into thumbnail sized cubes.
  2. Toss in large bowl with olive oil, lemon juice and pepper.
  3. Add salt, to taste, right before serving
