Shirazi Salad

Serves 7 - 10, as part of a larger meal


Description

Bright, vibrant and refreshing. This salad is known as Salad-e Shirazi in Iran and is a common pairing to many Iranian meals. It's made with simple and fresh ingredients and requires little prep. It's very important to get good quality English or Persian cucumbers, fresh crisp tomatoes, and onion. Be sure to chop all the vegetables into cubes roughly the same size.

Ingredients

  • 3 large English cucumbers or 8 smaller Persian cucumbers
  • 8 roma tomatoes or 4 - 5 larger tomatoes
  • 1 fresh red onions
  • 3 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • One or two handfuls of chopped flat-leaf parsley, most stems removed
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly cracked black pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Chop all vegetables into equal sized sized cubes, roughly the diameter of a nickel.
  2. Toss in large bowl with olive oil, lemon juice and cracked pepper to taste.
  3. Add salt, to taste, just before serving
